    INSTRUCTIONS

  • The current application is only supported in chrome v-33 and greater(only desktop).
  • For better performance of application use headphone/earphone.
  • To start query with Jarvis , say any of following command =>
  • ...HOW ARE YOU JARVIS....
  • ...SWITCH ON JARVIS....
  • ...WHAT ARE YOU DOING JARVIS....

    • Play the video

  • To play video say =>... PLAY (song/video name)
  • To remove video say => ...REMOVE... or ...STOP...

    • Query the questions

  • To ask question use questions starting with question words. Ex= (WHO/WHAT/HOW/WHICH.. etc) is APJ Abdul Kalam?
  • To go to conversation section say => ...go to the CONVERSATION section..
  • To come back to information section say=> ...go to the INFORMATION section
  • To go full screen say => ...go to (FULL/WIDE) SCREEN ...
  • To go back to smaller screen say => ...make the screen (NARROW/SMALL) ...
  • To clear screen say => (CLEAR ALL/REMOVE) ...

    • Shut down Jarvis from Query

  • to stop jarvis from queying say => ...SHUT DOWN Jarvis...
  • To start the jarvis again follow the start section shown above
    • Setting
  • To open setting say =>...open the SETTING Jarvis...
  • To close the setting say => ...close the SETTING Jarvis...
    • Instructions
  • To open the instructions say => ...open the INSTRUCTIONS Jarvis...
  • To close the instructions say => ...close the INSTRUCTIONS Jarvis...

    • OTHER IMPORTANT NOTES

  • To go from 'question query section' to video section or vice versa you have to clear that section first (remove or clear as instructed in their respective section).
  • Jarvis can not recognise you well if their is clashes of voice in surrounding or their is a weak internet connection.
  • To enjoy fully press F11
  • You can use your own words in place of dots. only bold texts are the key words jarvis use to recognise the type of query.
